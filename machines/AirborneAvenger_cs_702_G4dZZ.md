#### Switch or software setup

- 5-Ball for PinGolf: Prog SW2 Toggle 1 OFF
- 3-ball for other formats: Prog SW2 Toggle 1 ON
- Disable extra balls
- Special set to points: Prog SW2 Toggle 8 ON
- 50K Replay Score: Rotary Switch to 0, Prog SW1 Toggle 4 OFF
- Max 20 credits: Prog SW1 Toggle 5 ON and Prog SW1 Toggle 6 ON
- Spellout reward set to points: Prog SW1 Toggle 2 ON

Source: [WA Pinball Machine Notes](http://wapinball.net/setups/)
