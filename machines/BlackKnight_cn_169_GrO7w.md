#### Concerns

Black Knight can potentially be a long playing game in a tournament environment with high-caliber players.

#### Physical setup

- Level machine - factory recommendation
- Outlane posts on "Easy Settings". I.e. least open.
- Ear plug tilt hack
- Verify tilt sensitivity. Should be sensitive, but only one danger with each hard bump
- Check flipper strength (after #43 setting below)
- Clean playfield if necessary
- Clean glass
- Verify volume

Source: [PAPA/ReplayFX Competition Notes](https://replayfoundation.org/papa/learning-center/director-guide/game-notes/#GameNotes) and [WA Pinball Machine Notes](http://wapinball.net/setups/)
