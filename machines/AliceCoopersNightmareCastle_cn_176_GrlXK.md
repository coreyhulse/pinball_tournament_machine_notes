#### Concerns

- Locked balls stolen by a player result in the player who originally locked the ball receiving points. This has a small chance of creating an opportunity for collusion. As a result you should consider playing this as a single-player game.
- The game does not have any tilt through protection and if setup with a tight tilt it is possible for a single move to tilt-through multiple players. Consider using an earplug in the tilt hook to prevent this issue.
