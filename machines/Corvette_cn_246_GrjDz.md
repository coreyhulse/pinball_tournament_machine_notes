#### Tech Notes

Be sure the flippers are strong enough to make all ramps easily prior to the tournament starting

#### Physical setup

- Level Machine
- Outlane Posts on "Hard Settings". I.e. most open.
- Check if flippers go dead on coin door open. If yes, disable coin door switch.
- Verify Tilt Sensitivity
- Check Flipper Strength

Source: [PAPA/ReplayFX Competition Notes](https://replayfoundation.org/papa/learning-center/director-guide/game-notes/#GameNotes) and [WA Pinball Machine Notes](http://wapinball.net/setups/)
