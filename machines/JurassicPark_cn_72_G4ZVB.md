#### Game Version

- This is the version by Data East, 1993

#### Tournament Mode

- Expo or PAPA (sets free play, match off, replay off, high score off)
- IFPA (sets coin play default pricing, match off, replay off, high score off)
- Do Tournament mode first as this resets many settings

#### Custom ROM

- A v6.00 ROM is available that balances scoring, fixes bugs, and introduces new settings
