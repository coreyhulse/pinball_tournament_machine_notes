#### Concerns

The kickback award can be "Gift from the Gods", which gives all other players 1,000,000 points. To avoid concerns about its impact on multiplayer competition this game can be played as a single player game or you can disable the switch. Since this is an equal benefit to all other players it can be OK to play this as a multiplayer game, but the risk of triggering this feature means that other players' positions are not fixed until after the entire game is over.
