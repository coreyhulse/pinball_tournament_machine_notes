#### Concerns

Expert players will exploit the infinite showdown multiball possibility

#### Physical setup

- Level machine
- Outlane posts on "Hard Settings". I.e. most open.
- Verify Tilt Sensitivity
- Check coin door killing flippers.
- Check flipper strength
